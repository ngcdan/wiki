### Logistics Terminology

#### POL (Port of Loading)
Port where goods are loaded onto the vessel.

#### POD (Port of Discharge)
Port where goods are unloaded from the vessel.

#### Shipping Note
Document issued by carrier to confirm shipment details.

#### Manifest
Complete list of cargo on a vessel.

#### Packing List
Detailed list of items in each package/container.

#### CRD (Cargo Release Date)
Date when cargo is available for release.

#### ETA (Estimated Time of Arrival)
Expected arrival time at destination.

#### ETD (Estimated Time of Departure)
Expected departure time from origin.
