## Bài tập: Weather Vocabulary

### Từ vựng chỉ nhiệt độ
- Temperature: Nhiệt độ
- Warm: Ấm áp
- Hot: Nóng
- Cold: Lạnh
- Chilly: Lạnh thấu xương

### Từ vựng chỉ tình trạng thời tiết
- Rain/Rainy, Wind/Windy, Cloud/Cloudy, Fog/Foggy
- Ice/Icy, Snow/Snowy, Storm/Stormy

### Từ vựng chỉ hiện tượng thời tiết
- Thunderstorm: Bão kèm sấm sét
- Flood/Heavy Flood: Lũ lụt
- Tornado: Lốc xoáy

### Mẹo
- Danh từ thêm "y" → tính từ: Rain → Rainy, Wind → Windy

## Bài tập: Describe Weather in Hanoi

**Bản dịch của tôi:**
> I come from Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam. There are four seasons in a year here.
> In spring, the weather is very warm. The weather is very hot in the summer.
> At the end of summer, there are many thunderstorms, this causes heavy floods and even tornadoes.
> In autumn, there are many clouds. Sometimes in the morning, it's foggy.
> During the winter, it's very cool and dry in Hanoi. It's even chilly in December.
> But it isn't icy or snowy as other countries like England and American.

**Phiên bản sửa:**
> I come from Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam. There are four seasons here.
> In spring, the weather is very warm. The weather gets very hot in summer.
> At the end of summer, there are many thunderstorms, which cause heavy flooding and even tornadoes.
> In autumn, it's often cloudy, and sometimes it's foggy in the morning.
> During winter, it's cold and dry in Hanoi, and it's even freezing in December.
> But it isn't as icy or snowy as in countries like England or the United States.

## Grammar Notes

Để trả lời câu hỏi "đã làm chưa/không":
- Trả lời: **I haven't**
- Ví dụ: "Hey, did you read the latest edition of that comic?" → "No, I haven't. Is it good?"
